About CAATE

Creative Aesthetics Academy and Technical Education Inc. is a premier TESDA-accredited institution dedicated to producing world-class beauty and aesthetic professionals. Our comprehensive programs combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical training.

We take pride in our state-of-the-art facilities, expert instructors, and industry-aligned curriculum that prepares students for successful careers in the beauty and wellness industry.
