About CAATE

Creative Aesthetics Academy and Technical Education Inc. is a premier TESDA-accredited institution dedicated to producing world-class beauty and aesthetic professionals. Our comprehensive programs combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical training.

From foundational beauty care to advanced aesthetic services, CAATE delivers structured training and accredited assessments aligned with national standards and global industry expectations.

Our Mission

“CAATE is dedicated to delivering world-class training that empowers individuals with the knowledge, skills, and expertise to excel in beauty enhancement and professional development. We foster a culture of innovation, mastery, and continuous growth, preparing confident, industry-ready graduates who can lead and transform the beauty sector.”

Our Vision
